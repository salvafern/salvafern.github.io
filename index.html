  • Development and maintenance of R Shiny apps.
  • Creation of GIS data products in the framework of three projects: marineregions.org, bio-oracle.org, and the Biology lot of emodnet.eu.

Publication of datasets via Geoserver and ERDDAP. Metadata management in several systems such as GeoNetwork or GBIF IPT. Creation of DOIs.
